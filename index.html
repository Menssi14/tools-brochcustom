Service · No. 01

Custom embroidery

What we stitch on

  • Apparel caps, polos, jackets, hoodies, work shirts
  • Bags & goods totes, backpacks, duffels, blankets
  • Patches sew-on, heat-transfer glue, or velcro backing
  • Specialty graduation stoles, sashes, aprons

Bring your own items or order through us — we’ll match the stitching to whatever you’ve got.

How it works

  1. Send your design — a sketch, photo, screenshot, or rough idea. We’ll help shape it.
  2. We send a mockup and full quote.
  3. 50% deposit to start production.
  4. Pickup or delivery, balance on completion.

Embroidery is our premium option — built to last decades through wash and wear. One-time $10 design fee per new logo turns your artwork into a stitch file, reusable for every future order.

Service · No. 02

Apparel printing

What we print on

  • Apparel t-shirts, hoodies, sweatshirts, jerseys, jackets
  • Caps sleeve and front prints — sharp full-color art
  • Drinkware mugs, tumblers, water bottles
  • Hard goods phone cases, mousepads, coasters, photo panels

Bring your own apparel or order through us.

Also — heat-transfer vinyl

  • HTV jersey names & numbers, simple solid-color designs
  • Specialty glitter, metallic, and holographic finishes

How it works

  1. Send your design — sketch, photo, or just an idea. We’ll help shape it.
  2. Free digital mockup and quote.
  3. 50% deposit to start.
  4. Printed, pressed, and ready for pickup or delivery.

Printing is our most affordable decoration method — great for full-color designs, big team runs, and budgets that don’t bend. We use full-color heat press for any fabric and dye press for polyester apparel and coated drinkware. Both are wash-tough and won’t crack or peel.

Service · No. 03

Custom engraving

What we engrave

  • Drinkware tumblers, cups, mugs, water bottles
  • Small goods pens, metal keychains, dog tags, business cards
  • Wood cutting boards, plaques, signage, ornaments
  • Leather & metal wallets, journals, belts, knives, awards

Bring your own item or order through us. Not sure if it’ll work? Send a photo and we’ll confirm before you commit.

Also — cut vinyl

  • Adhesive vinyl stickers, decals, business signage, window graphics

How it works

  1. Send your design and the item or material. We’ll help shape the art.
  2. We send a mockup and quote.
  3. 50% deposit to start.
  4. Finished, polished, and ready.

We engrave nearly anything. Wood, leather, metal, glass, painted surfaces — if it can be marked, we can probably mark it. From a single gift to a batch of awards.
