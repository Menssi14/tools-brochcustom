From the workshop

Four trades, one shop.

No. 01

Embroidery

Stitched thread artwork on caps, polos, jackets, and bags. Clean lines, whether it’s a single piece or a full team order.

No. 02

DTF printing

A modern way to put bright, full-color designs on fabric. The art is printed onto film, then heat-pressed onto a t-shirt or hoodie — soft to wear, sharp to look at, and built to last through wash after wash.

No. 03

Laser engraving

Precise, permanent marks on tumblers, wood, leather, and acrylic. Great for gifts, signage, and small-batch branding.

No. 04

Sublimation

Full-color dye printing for light polyester apparel, mugs, and hard goods. Vivid, photo-quality results that won’t crack or peel.

Design services included as needed

Need help with the artwork? We’ll redraw a sketch, clean up a logo, or scale your file up to print-ready — for any of the trades above.
